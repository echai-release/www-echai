\f0\fs24 \cf0 The Real Shift GenAI Enables in Revenue Ops: Authority\

\

GenAI only changes sales when it does three things managers cannot do at scale.\

\

This is not about productivity or automation.\

It\'92s about authority.\

\

1. Externalizing Truth\

\

Sales decisions are often made using internal signals:\

CRM activity, rep notes, meeting summaries.\

\

GenAI expands the frame by continuously reading external reality:\

\

company strategy and positioning\

\

hiring and org changes\

\

funding and financial signals\

\

product launches and regulatory pressure\

\

customer language and market behavior\

\

Because this data is continuously monitored, it establishes a new baseline.\

\

The core question shifts from:\

\'93What did the rep do?\'94\

to\

\'93Is this company behaving like a buyer right now?\'94\

\

2. Normalizing Judgment\

\

Today, deal judgment is subjective and personal.\

\

When managers disagree with reps, it creates friction and inconsistency.\

\

GenAI makes judgment procedural.\

\

Instead of relying on opinion, the system evaluates evidence and states:\

\'93This is not a buying moment because key conditions are missing.\'94\

\

This reduces emotional conflict and increases consistency across teams.\

\

3. Making Uncertainty Explicit\

\

Uncertainty is usually hidden in sales workflows.\

\

Deals remain in commit even when confidence is low.\

\

GenAI can explicitly label uncertainty:\

\

unproven deals\

\

missing stakeholders\

\

deals revived due to changed external conditions\

\

This behavior is new.\

Most systems avoid it because it feels risky.\

\

But it is necessary for trust.\

\

The Future Way of Working\

\

With GenAI:\

\

managers ask systems for second opinions\

\

forecast calls focus on evidence, not narratives\

\

reps are asked to close gaps, not defend optimism\

\

pipeline hygiene becomes continuous\

\

The biggest shift is simple:\

\

Deal reality becomes something you can ask for \'97 not something you argue about.\

\

That is the real value GenAI brings to revenue operations.\

\

Authority.
