What is LoopX?

LoopX is a revenue intelligence system that evaluates deal reality using internal and external signals.

  • Call transcripts
  • Meeting notes
  • CRM context
  • User corrections
  • External company signals: hiring, leadership shifts, strategic pivots, and funding changes

LoopX does not generate surface-level summaries. It validates whether a deal reflects real buying conditions or hope.

Who is LoopX built for?

LoopX is built primarily for sales managers, directors of sales, VPs of sales, and CROs.

If your forecast conversations include questions like ‚ÄúWhat changed?‚Äù, ‚ÄúWho is the buyer?‚Äù, ‚ÄúWhy now?‚Äù, and ‚ÄúIs this real?‚Äù, LoopX is built for you.

Does LoopX replace CRM?

No. CRM records activity. LoopX evaluates meaning.

CRM answers ‚ÄúWhat happened?‚Äù LoopX answers ‚ÄúWhat does this actually indicate?‚Äù

Why does LoopX focus on external signals?

Buying decisions do not happen inside your CRM. They happen inside the customer organization where priorities, budgets, and leadership can shift.

LoopX evaluates whether the outside world supports the internal narrative so forecast confidence is based on reality.

üß† Technology & Architecture

Is LoopX just an LLM wrapper?

No.

An LLM wrapper generates text. LoopX is an agentic decision system that ingests structured and unstructured signals, tracks contradictions, versions facts, manages session state, enforces execution guardrails, and surfaces decision gaps.

The language model is one component. The architecture is the product.

Is LoopX predictive AI?

Not yet. Today LoopX is evidence-based, not prediction-based.

  • What signals exist
  • What signals are missing
  • What changed externally
  • What conflicts internally

As signal history compounds, LoopX is building the foundation for predictive intelligence.
