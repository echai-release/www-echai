Launch Announcement - Resurrect Your Dead Deals with LoopX

Resurrect your closed-lost deals with LoopX’s CSV-based MVP: quick, no integrations, and designed to identify warm re-engagement opportunities.

Access it here

by Pari Ambatkar • December 11, 2025

Sales teams lose deals for many reasons. Budget disappears. A champion leaves. A feature gap shows up at the wrong time. The deal gets marked Closed Lost and buried. Most AEs never look at these accounts again, even when things change and the timing becomes right.

LoopX changes that with a simple workflow. Upload a CSV of your Closed Lost deals and LoopX tells you which ones are ready for a callback and why.

This is our teaser MVP. It is designed to give you quick value with almost no setup. No integrations. No long steps. No dashboards. You upload a basic CSV and you get a short report showing the deals worth resurrecting. A full version is coming soon, and we want your feedback to shape it.

How it works

You export your Closed Lost opportunities from Salesforce or any CRM. You upload the CSV into LoopX. We scan each account through external signals. Leadership changes that create new champions. Financial changes that unlock budget. Product and stack changes that fix what blocked you before.

You get a simple report with short, punchy lines that tell you exactly what changed.

  • “They raised a Series B and hired a new CFO.”
  • “Your champion left and a new VP joined last month.”
  • “They consolidated vendors and now your product fits better.”

You decide who to call. LoopX gives you the timing and the reason.

This MVP is built for speed and learning. Perfect for Series A to Series C teams. Ideal for mid market AEs who run full cycle and want more warm pipeline without heavy tooling.

Tell us what works. Tell us what needs to be simpler. Tell us what signals matter most to your deals. Your feedback will directly influence the full version, which will add deeper signals, more automation, and optional Salesforce syncing.

Try the MVP today. Upload your CSV of dead deals. See which ones are alive again. At the end of the flow, join the waitlist for the full version and help shape the next stage of LoopX.
